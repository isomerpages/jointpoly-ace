<h3><u>April 2025</u></h3>
<h4>Webinar: MOE-TRF Grant Writing</h4>
<p>This webinar was jointly organised by MOE, the Joint Polytechnic Education
Research Community of Practice and JP-AcE.</p>
<p><strong>Speakers</strong>
<br>Professor Bernard Tan, Senior Vice Provost at the National University
of Singapore (NUS) gave 3 tips on writing a successful grant application.
He then had an open Q&amp;A session with participants. Ms Shirley Tan from
MOE addressed the more procedural aspects of the grant application process.</p>
